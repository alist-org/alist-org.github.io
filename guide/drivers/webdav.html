                                                          # OneDrive/SharePoint

                                                          Select the vendor as sharepoint, and support the international version/21Vianet.

                                                          For OneDrive/SharePoint, you can obtain the webdav root address through this toolopen in new window. If you want to mount the specified directory, you can join it later.

                                                          The username is the OneDrive account email, and the password is the OneDrive account password.
